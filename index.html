How It All Started

The Best Ideas Start with a Happy Accident!

What happens when creativity meets a little chaos?

It started with a simple coffee spill. Our lead designer was flipping through a makeup catalog when—oops!—a cup of coffee splashed across the pages, blending colors in a way that looked... stunning! That’s when we thought: ‘What if we could create a game that turns beauty and color into a fun, engaging puzzle?’
